Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is crucial for building a prosperous and self-reliant Bangladesh, driving economic growth and creating opportunities.

Sustainability

Sustainability is vital for ensuring a balanced and prosperous future for Bangladesh by preserving natural resources and protecting the environment.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the backbone of a modern Bangladesh, enabling economic growth and improved quality of life.

Empowerment

Empowerment is essential for building an inclusive and equitable Bangladesh, enabling individuals to reach their full potential.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus is renowned for his concept of the "Three Zeros"— Zero Poverty, Zero Unemployment, and Zero Net Carbon Emissions.


1. On Zero Poverty: "We can put poverty in the museums of the future. It has no place in the twenty-first century."


2. On Zero Unemployment: "Human beings are not job seekers. They are job creators by nature. We just need to create a system that unleashes their entrepreneurial spirit."


3. On Zero Net Carbon Emissions: "Businesses must be designed to solve problems, not create them. Social business can lead us to a world with zero net carbon emissions."


Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
