The Vision of Future

Vision of Future: A New Bangladesh The vision for a new Bangladesh is centered around transforming the nation into a model of sustainable development, economic prosperity, and social harmony. By harnessing the potential of its people and resources, Bangladesh aims to emerge as a global leader in innovation and resilience.
Key Pillars of the Vision:

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus is renowned for his concept of the "Three Zeros"— Zero Poverty, Zero Unemployment, and Zero Net Carbon Emissions.


1. On Zero Poverty: "We can put poverty in the museums of the future. It has no place in the twenty-first century."


2. On Zero Unemployment: "Human beings are not job seekers. They are job creators by nature. We just need to create a system that unleashes their entrepreneurial spirit."


3. On Zero Net Carbon Emissions: "Businesses must be designed to solve problems, not create them. Social business can lead us to a world with zero net carbon emissions."
